Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  TX Contract Intel

TX Contract Intel ("we," "us," or "our") operates txcontractintel.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you subscribe, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your weekly digest and account communications
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Basic usage data — email open and click events so we can improve the digest

How We Use Your Information

  • To send your weekly contract digest (the core service)
  • To process your subscription payment via Stripe
  • To send service-related emails (receipts, account updates)
  • To improve the product based on aggregate usage patterns

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not send you marketing emails unrelated to the service you subscribed to
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Supabase — secure data storage

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing their use of data.

Data Retention

We retain your email address and subscription status for as long as your account is active. If you cancel, we remove your email from active digest lists within 7 days. You may request full deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Unsubscribe from the digest using the link at the bottom of any email
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@txcontractintel.com.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising pixels.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we'll notify you by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions? Email support@txcontractintel.com.
